### Week 3 Objectives:
- Gain in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience with the AWS Budgets cost management service to establish account monitoring mechanisms.
- Activate automatic budget alerts to control internship credit costs.
- Initialize a test image storage environment for the resources (pet images, service invoices) of the Pet Resort project using Amazon S3.
